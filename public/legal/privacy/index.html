Privacy Policy

Synapse Learning · Effective 6 July 2026 · App ID com.synapse.learning

Contact: privacy@synapse-learning.io

1. What we collect

Account data: email, hashed password (or Google OAuth subject), subscription plan, Stripe customer ID when applicable, API usage counters.

Study content: uploads, courses, notes, quiz sessions, workspace state — stored locally and synced when signed in.

Institution data: org name, rosters, assignments, grades, LTI/SAML identifiers, audit logs for admin actions.

Automatic: device type, app version, security/rate-limit logs; optional crash reports if Sentry is enabled.

AI processing: excerpts may be sent to configured upstream providers (e.g. OpenAI). We do not use your content to train third-party models unless you separately opt in.

2. How we use data

  • Provide and improve the study workspace, sync, and institution features
  • Authenticate users and enforce org RBAC
  • Bill paid plans and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations and school audit requests

We do not sell personal information.

4. Sharing

Infrastructure providers, Stripe (billing), AI upstream APIs you configure, and your institution when using school SSO.

5. Retention

Active accounts: while the account exists. Deleted accounts: purged within 30 days unless legal hold. Audit logs: 24 months for institution tenants.

6. Your rights

Access, correction, deletion, portability, or restriction depending on jurisdiction. Institution students: contact your school administrator first.

7. Children & schools

Schools act as independent controllers for roster and gradebook data. We process student data on the school's instructions under our DPA.

8. Security

TLS in transit, encrypted Postgres at rest (deployment-dependent), tenant isolation, JWT auth with refresh rotation, Redis rate limits in production.

9. Changes

Updates posted at this URL with a new effective date. Material institution changes notified to org admins.
